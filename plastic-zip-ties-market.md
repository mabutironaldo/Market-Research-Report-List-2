PA12 zip ties are known for their flexibility and resistance to moisture. PA46 zip ties are made of a specialty nylon with superior strength and resistance to abrasion. PP zip ties are made of polypropylene, offering good chemical resistance. PE zip ties are made of polyethylene, known for its flexibility and resistance to moisture. PTFE zip ties are made of a fluoropolymer material known for its chemical resistance and high temperature tolerance. The "others" category includes various types of zip ties made from different materials or blends.</p></p>
