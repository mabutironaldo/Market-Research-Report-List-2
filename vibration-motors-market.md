<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Vibration motors are categorized into three types based on their construction: moving-coil, moving-iron, and moving-magnet. </p><p>Moving-coil motors have a coil attached to a vibrating object, producing vibrations when current flows through the coil. </p><p>Moving-iron motors use an iron mass attached to a coil, causing vibrations as the coil moves in relation to the iron. </p><p>Moving-magnet motors have a magnet attached to a vibrating object, generating vibrations as the magnet moves back and forth due to an external magnetic field. These types cater to different applications and offer various performance characteristics in the vibration motors market.</p></p>
