<p><p>The membrane filtration systems market consists of various types including microfiltration (MF), ultrafiltration (UF), nanofiltration (NF), and reverse osmosis (RO). </p><p>Microfiltration utilizes a porous membrane to separate particles and microorganisms from liquids. </p><p>Ultrafiltration is used to separate larger particles, colloids, and macromolecules. </p><p>Nanofiltration is employed to remove smaller molecules, including divalent ions. </p><p>Reverse osmosis applies pressure to separate dissolved solutes from water by passing it through a semi-permeable membrane. These technologies find applications in industries such as water and wastewater treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
