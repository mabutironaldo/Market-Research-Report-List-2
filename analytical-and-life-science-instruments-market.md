<p><p>Analytical and life science instruments market includes various types of instruments used in scientific research and analysis. Chromatography instruments are used to separate mixtures into individual components. Electrophoresis instruments are used to separate and analyze proteins, DNA, and other molecules based on their size and charge. Microscopy instruments allow scientists to view and study objects that are too small to be seen by the naked eye. Spectroscopy instruments are used to analyze the interaction of light with matter. PCR (polymerase chain reaction) instruments are used to amplify and detect DNA sequences. Cell counting instruments are used to quantify the number of cells in a sample. Other instruments include flow cytometers, mass spectrometers, and centrifuges.</p></p>
