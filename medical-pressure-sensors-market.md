<p><p>Medical pressure sensors are devices used to measure the pressure within the human body for monitoring and diagnosis purposes. There are two types of medical pressure sensors: MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) based sensors and pressure transducers. MEMS based sensors are miniaturized devices that use tiny silicon-based structures to measure and transmit pressure data. They are commonly used in applications like blood pressure monitoring. Pressure transducers, on the other hand, are larger devices that convert pressure into an electrical signal, commonly used in medical equipment such as ventilators or infusion pumps.</p></p>
