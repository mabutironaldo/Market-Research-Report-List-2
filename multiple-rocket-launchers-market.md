They are commonly used for rapid deployment and quick response.</p><p>Towed rocket launchers are lighter and portable. They require a separate vehicle or platform to transport and set up. They are typically used for static positions and require more time for deployment.</p><p>Overall, these multiple rocket launchers market types cater to different operational requirements, providing flexibility and tactical advantages to military forces.</p></p>
