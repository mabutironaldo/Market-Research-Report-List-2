<p><p>The License Plate Recognition System Market consists of three major types: Mobile License Plate Recognition (LPR) System, Fixed LPR System, and Portable LPR System. </p><p>The Mobile LPR System is mounted on vehicles, enabling real-time scanning and identification of license plates while on the move. The Fixed LPR System is installed at fixed locations like toll booths or parking lots, capturing license plate information of stationary vehicles. The Portable LPR System is a handheld device which provides flexibility to law enforcement officers for license plate scanning in various locations.</p></p>
