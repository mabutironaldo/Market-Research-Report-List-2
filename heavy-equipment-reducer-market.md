<p><p>The heavy equipment reducer market is categorized into four types: travel reducer, rotary reducer, hoist reducer, and push-pressure reducer. The travel reducer is used to control the speed and power transmission of a vehicle, such as excavators and bulldozers. Rotary reducers are employed in rotating machinery like cranes and drilling rigs. Hoist reducers are utilized in lifting and lowering mechanisms, such as elevators and hoists. Push-pressure reducers control the pushing force in heavy equipment, enabling efficient performance. These different types cater to specific applications in the heavy equipment industry.</p></p>
