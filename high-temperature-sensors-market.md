<p><p>High Temperature Sensors market comprises three types: Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD), Thermocouple, and Semiconductor-based Sensors. RTDs work on the principle of electrical resistance change in metals with temperature variations. Thermocouples generate voltage based on the temperature difference between two metal junctions. Semiconductor-based Sensors employ semiconductor materials to measure temperature changes via electrical resistance alterations. These sensors are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and power generation, where accurate and reliable temperature measurements are vital for process control and safety purposes.</p></p>
